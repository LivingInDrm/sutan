### 推荐（三选一定版）

**v2.1 baseline ➡ 候选 B3（新仙侠插画）**
- warm_signal 0.0（中位数 -0.014），远超 <0.10 目标，五角色全部 < 0.11
- 五角色全部保留武侠身份和武器，仙气 + 干净度双最强
- 唯一能硬指标稳定达标的候选

**并行保留 B1（水墨主导）** 作为"水墨风卡面/宣发图"的风格分支

**B4（半写实厚涂）不进入 baseline，建议降级为"纯艺术参考"**
- warm_signal 0.156 超出 <0.10 目标 56%，**未达标**
- 风格本身非常好（电影级厚涂、真实光影、重量感，确实最贴"雪中悍刀行江湖武侠"气质）
- 但 B4 的核心画法（"cinematic + painterly + detailed fabric"）与模型训练先验里"西方厚涂插画 / 黑神话暖棕色调"强耦合；即使 negative 里明确 no yellow / no sepia，模型仍会在皮肤、地面影子、旧布料、兵器上反复加入暖棕 —— 这是风格内生的色温偏好，难以仅靠负面词压制
- 详见 §3.4 和 §4.3 的肉眼评估：B4 的单张效果非常出彩，但 warm_signal 分布极紧（std 0.049）且均值稳定在 0.13-0.20，说明**不是随机偏差，而是风格底色**。想真正用 B4 风格需要下一轮单独再调（例如 "cold cinematic lighting" / "moonlit palette" 等反向锚点），不是单次迭代能修好的

### 不达标与风险（诚实告知）

- **B3 老黄 warm_signal 0.104** 略高：根因是"老黄/weary"角色描述里仍把模型往 warm palette 拽（飘带米白+暖金），并非 style_base 的问题
  - 行动建议：Phase B2 对 description 层做"色温敏感词清扫"（weary/rakish/sunset/golden 等），B3 可进一步下降到均值 <0
  - 现在不处理也能直接进生产，因 B3 其余 4/5 角色全部进入 cool 区
- **B1 温华 / 老黄 warm_signal 0.15~0.16**：同类"角色设定本身偏暖"问题，Phase B2 一并处理
- **B4 全部角色 > 0.10**：如用户坚持厚涂风，需要**单独开 Phase A'**，不要和 B3 同场对比
- **结构可靠性**：60 张图（20×3），**0 张**人物解剖崩坏，武器/服饰/姿态全部保留

### 是否需要再一轮 iteration？

**对 B3/B1 不需要**：已定版就绪
- B3 超额达标，直接作为 v2.1 baseline 合并到生产 templates.json

### 候选 B1：Ink-wash + Color Hybrid（水墨 + 彩绘融合）
**设计策略**：
- 主体保留 A2 的"干净彩绘 + 白底"底色，但把"flat digital illustration"替换成"clean watercolor body"
- 水墨元素限定在 **garments（衣摆）+ background wisps（飞白留白）**，绝不全图做旧
- 明确加入 "airy" / "sparse" / "flying-white (feibai)" / "clean negative space" 去对抗"堆积/做旧"
- portrait_template 里追加一句硬约束：身体 clean watercolor，**飞白只作气氛点缀**

**最终 style_base**：
```
Modern wuxia character illustration blended with light ink-wash accents, crisp watercolor body rendering with clean linework on the figure itself, flowing ink splash and airy brush strokes only on garments and background wisps, airy and sparse ink-wash composition with flying-white (feibai) brushwork and plenty of clean negative space, bright clear atmosphere with neutral to slightly cool color temperature, saturated translucent colors on pure white background, realistic human proportions, elegant full-body figure, restrained composition with generous negative space, transparent background, no background scenery, clean blank backdrop, clean and focused single-character composition
```

文件：`tools/asset-manager/prompts/experiments/v2_B1_templates.json`

### 候选 B3：Modern Xianxia Illustration（新仙侠插画）⭐
**设计策略**：
- 核心词从 "modern Chinese illustration" 升级到 **"modern xianxia"**（更锁仙侠定位）
- 加入 "ethereal fantasy atmosphere" + "soft rim light" + "subtle qi aura" —— 仙气 + 柔光 = 看起来自然清透
- 加入 "flowing silk garments and fluttering fabric" —— 锁定飘逸/飞动的动态感
- "translucent digital coloring with refined cel-shading" —— 替代 A2 的 "flat shading"，避免扁平
- 追加 negative：`no antique look / no dim lighting / no gloomy mood / no muddy colors / no flat boring shading / no modern casual wear`

**最终 style_base**：
```
Modern xianxia character illustration, ethereal fantasy atmosphere with soft rim light and subtle qi aura, crisp sharp linework with flowing silk garments and fluttering fabric, translucent digital coloring with refined cel-shading, bright clear luminous atmosphere with neutral to slightly cool color temperature, saturated clean colors on pure white background, realistic human proportions, elegant full-body figure, restrained composition with generous negative space, transparent background, no background scenery, clean blank backdrop, clean and focused single-character composition
```

文件：`tools/asset-manager/prompts/experiments/v2_B3_templates.json`

### 候选 B4：Semi-realistic Chinese Digital Painting（国风厚涂半写实）
**设计策略**：
- 核心词 **"semi-realistic digital painting"** + "cinematic painterly rendering" —— 锁定电影级厚涂定位
- "dramatic soft rim light and volumetric key lighting" —— 戏剧性打光（降低平面感）
- "detailed fabric textures with believable cloth weight and folds" —— 强调布料重量与真实褶皱（黑神话/遮天风的关键）
- "rich deep saturated palette that stays neutral to slightly cool in temperature" —— 主动抗暖调锚点
- 追加 negative：`no photorealism portrait photo / no photo reference look / no flat shading / no cel-shading / no anime flat look / no muddy dark palette / no dim gloomy lighting / no heavy grunge / no torn canvas`

**最终 style_base**：
```
Semi-realistic Chinese wuxia character digital painting, cinematic painterly rendering with dramatic soft rim light and volumetric key lighting, detailed fabric textures with believable cloth weight and folds, confident painterly brushwork with crisp character focus, rich deep saturated palette that stays neutral to slightly cool in temperature, clean bright key light on the figure against pure white background, realistic human proportions and anatomy, elegant full-body figure with tangible physical presence, restrained composition with generous negative space, transparent background, no background scenery, clean blank backdrop, clean and focused single-character composition
```

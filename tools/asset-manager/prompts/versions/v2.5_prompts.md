# v2.5 prompts — B4-Lean (precision-budget variant)

**Date**: 2026-04-18
**Motivation**: Redistribute attention budget from boilerplate style directives to the per-character `description`, specifically to improve facial/eye clarity without altering style DNA.
**Hypothesis**: B4 spent ~12% of prompt tokens on the description and ~88% on largely redundant style/negative boilerplate. A leaner scaffold, combined with an explicit FACE/EYE directive, should shift attention back onto the description (and faces in particular).
**Scope**: New template file only. B4 is untouched.
**Evaluation**: manual, by the user. No automated metrics in this archive.

- New file: `tools/asset-manager/prompts/experiments/v2_B4-Lean_templates.json`
- B4 baseline: `tools/asset-manager/prompts/experiments/v2_B4_templates.json`
- Side-by-side HTML: `tools/asset-manager/generated/portrait-eval/B4_vs_B4-Lean_20260418.html`
- 6 Lean images + prompts + descriptions: `tools/asset-manager/generated/portrait-eval/B4-Lean_20260418/`

## 1. Diff (B4 → B4-Lean)

### 1.1 `style_base`: 88 → 54 words (−34 / −39%)

**Kept verbatim (style DNA):**
- `Semi-realistic Chinese wuxia character digital painting`
- `cinematic painterly rendering with dramatic soft rim light and volumetric key lighting`
- `detailed fabric textures with believable cloth weight and folds`
- `confident painterly brushwork with crisp character focus`
- `rich deep saturated palette that stays neutral to slightly cool in temperature`
- `realistic human proportions and anatomy`

**Removed (redundant with `portrait_template` composition line):**
- `clean bright key light on the figure against pure white background` — conflicts with transparent background
- `elegant full-body figure with tangible physical presence` — duplicated in Subject line
- `restrained composition with generous negative space` — duplicated in composition line
- `no background scenery, clean blank backdrop` — collapsed into `clean transparent background`
- `clean and focused single-character composition` — duplicated

### 1.2 `no_text_constraint`: 49 → 13 words (−36 / −73%)

Before: `CRITICAL REQUIREMENT: The image must be completely free of any text, letters, words, characters, writing systems, calligraphy, seals, stamps, chop marks, red seal marks, watermarks, signatures, inscriptions, or labels of any kind anywhere in the image. The image should contain ONLY the visual subject with no decorative text elements.`

After: `No text, letters, calligraphy, seals, stamps, watermarks, or signatures anywhere in the image.`

Rationale: the 3× longer form was insurance, not signal.

### 1.3 `portrait_template` scaffold: 93 → 63 words (−30 / −32%)

**Removed:**
- Hardcoded pose sentence `The character stands in a poised, story-driven martial arts pose with tangible weight and grounded footing…` — pose belongs in per-variant description / variant_hint, not the template (~22 words).
- `Rich and vibrant costume colors — each character has their own distinct color palette; painterly brush strokes sculpt fabric folds and armor/leather details, with a clean neutral rim light defining silhouette against pure white.` — ~34 words of pure duplication with `style_base`.
- `Transparent background, nothing else behind the character, clean blank negative space only.` — duplicated in composition line.

**Added — new `FACE AND EYES` directive (~25 words):**
```
FACE AND EYES: Render facial features with sharp clarity. Eyes must be
clearly defined with visible iris color, catchlight, and lash detail.
No smudged, blurred, or muddy faces.
```

### 1.4 `style_negative`: 100 → 68 words (−32 / −32%)

**Kept (top-signal negatives):**
- Cartoon/child: `no chibi, no oversized head`
- Style drift: `no photorealism, no western fantasy style, no 3D render, no oil painting`
- Flat: `no flat shading, no cel-shading`
- Composition (all 7): `no complex background, no multiple characters, no extra arms, no extra fingers, no cropped feet, no distorted anatomy`
- Warm-cast defence (core 3): `no yellow tint, no sepia tone, no warm color cast`
- Mood: `no muddy dark palette, no dim gloomy lighting`

**Dropped (low-signal / speculative):**
- `no Q-version` (redundant with chibi)
- `no photo reference look` (vague)
- `no anime flat look` (covered by flat/cel-shading)
- Seven "paper texture"-family negatives: `no aged paper, no ivory background, no cream background, no paper texture, no rice paper look, no classical Chinese painting yellowing, no gongbi-on-silk look` — Phase A pile-on
- `no heavy grunge, no torn canvas` — not observed in recent runs

**Added — 1 eye-specific negative:**
- `no blurry eyes, no smeared facial features, no muddy face`

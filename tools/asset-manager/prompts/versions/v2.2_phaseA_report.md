## 2. B3.2 / B4.2 templates.json 完整 diff

> 完整 prompt 归档见：`tools/asset-manager/prompts/versions/v2.2_prompts.md`

### 2.1 B3.2 vs B3 的三个关键改动

**设计策略**：在保持 B3 "modern xianxia / ethereal / qi aura" 冷色底盘不动的前提下，三个方向同时发力：

#### (a) 细节密度（style_base 加词 + portrait_template 整段新增）

B3 原：`crisp sharp linework with flowing silk garments and fluttering fabric`

B3.2 改：`crisp intricate linework with richly detailed silk garments, elaborate fabric folds and embroidered trim, ornate traditional accessories (jade pendants, silk sashes, hairpieces), individually rendered flowing hair strands`

**以及** portrait_template 新增整段：
> Costume must be densely detailed: visible fabric weave, layered inner and outer robes, embroidered cuffs and collars, waist sash with jade or knotted ornaments, period-accurate footwear; hair rendered strand by strand with silk ribbons or hairpieces where appropriate; weapons and props (swords, gourds, fans, scrolls) finely modeled with grip textures, tassels and engraved details.

#### (b) 去日漫化（style_negative 新增 11 个 anti-anime token + 面部硬约束）

新增 negative：
```
no anime face, no manga face, no big sparkly anime eyes, no small anime nose, no anime cheek blush,
no anime aesthetic, no Japanese anime style, no Japanese manga style,
no cel-shaded flat style, no flat cel-shading, no anime cel-shading,
no shoujo style, no isekai art style, no Genshin Impact style, no Honkai style
```

新增 style_base 替换：`translucent digital coloring with refined cel-shading` → `richly layered digital illustration with soft painterly shading and clean highlights (not flat cel-shading, not anime cel-shading)`

新增 portrait_template 硬约束：
> Adult mature proportions — this is NOT an anime character; facial structure should read as a real East Asian adult, not a stylized anime face.

#### (c) 强化中国传统（style_base 加 "Tang-Song dynasty hanfu / Chinese ink painting influence on drapery"）

新增：`rooted in Chinese traditional aesthetics` / `Tang-Song dynasty hanfu and wuxia silhouettes with Chinese ink painting influence on drapery and ribbon flow`

### 2.2 B4.2 vs B4 的三个关键改动

**设计策略**：B4 的"cinematic painterly"风格内生偏暖（黑神话/西方厚涂 priors），所以 B4.2 **在 style_base 层面就直接写死"cool / moonlit / silver-blue"**，不指望 negative 词对抗——要**从正面语义就锁冷**。

#### (a) 光源硬改月光冷蓝

B4 原：`cinematic painterly rendering with dramatic soft rim light and volumetric key lighting`

B4.2 改：`cool cinematic mood, moonlit cinematic lighting with silver-blue key light and teal-blue shadows, neutral-cool color grading and desaturated palette, steel-gray ambient light with soft cyan rim`

#### (b) 色板主导词锁冷

B4 原：`rich deep saturated palette that stays neutral to slightly cool in temperature`

B4.2 改：`rich deep palette that stays firmly cool in temperature (blue, teal, slate, silver, indigo dominant)`

#### (c) style_negative 清扫暖色（24 个新增 token）

新增：
```
no yellow, no yellow cast,
no warm lighting, no warm palette, no golden hour, no golden lighting, no sunset lighting, no sunset palette,
no sepia, no ochre, no amber, no amber light,
no firelight, no torch light, no candlelight, no dusk lighting, no campfire glow,
no earth-tone dominance, no ochre-dominant palette, no brown-dominant palette,
no tan skin cast, no orange skin, no orange tint, no warm skin lighting,
no dust haze, no dusty atmosphere
```

同时 portrait_template 新增段：
> Lighting is firmly COOL: moonlit silver-blue key light from above, soft cyan rim light on the silhouette, teal-blue shadow tones, neutral-cool midtones; absolutely no warm light, no sunset glow, no golden hour, no firelight — even on skin and fabric, shadows must fall cool-blue not warm-brown.
